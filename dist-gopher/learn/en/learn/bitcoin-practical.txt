Your First Bitcoin: A Practical Start
=====================================

en

Everything you need to know before sending your first transaction - wallet addresses, wallet types, reading transactions, fees, and self-custody.

Ready to use Bitcoin? This course covers the practical essentials -
from understanding wallet addresses and choosing the right wallet, to
reading transactions, calculating fees, and securing your coins with
self-custody.

No theory, no philosophy - just the hands-on knowledge you need to
send and receive Bitcoin safely.

Read on full site: https://learn.txid.uk/en/learn/bitcoin-practical/
