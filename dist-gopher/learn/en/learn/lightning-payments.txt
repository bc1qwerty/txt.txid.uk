Lightning Payments & Running a Node
===================================

en

How Bitcoin scales for everyday payments - the Lightning Network explained, plus why running your own full node matters.

Bitcoin's base layer processes about 7 transactions per second. The
Lightning Network scales that to millions. This course explains how
Lightning works, what running a full node means for your sovereignty,
and why both matter for Bitcoin's future as everyday money.

Read on full site: https://learn.txid.uk/en/learn/lightning-payments/
