Why Money Is Broken
===================

en

The history and structural failures of our monetary system - from the gold standard's demise to inflation, the Cantillon Effect, interest rate manipulation, and CBDCs.

Before you can understand why Bitcoin matters, you need to understand
why the current monetary system is failing. This course traces the
history of money from gold to fiat, exposes the hidden mechanisms of
inflation and wealth transfer, and introduces the economic framework
that explains it all.

Read on full site: https://learn.txid.uk/en/learn/money-broken/
