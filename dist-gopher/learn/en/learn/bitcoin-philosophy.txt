The Bitcoin Thesis
==================

en

Why Bitcoin exists and why it matters - freedom, privacy, property rights, sound money, savings, time preference, and the case against gold.

This course builds the philosophical and economic case for Bitcoin.
From economic freedom and financial privacy to property rights, sound
money, and the ultimate endgame of hyperbitcoinization - each article
deepens your understanding of why Bitcoin is not just technology, but
a civilizational shift.
