What is Money? Let's hear a story!
----------------------------------

## What did we learn?

Long ago, people tried to trade things directly - this is called
bartering. But if the other person doesn't want what you have, you
can't trade! So people agreed to use something everyone liked as
money. With money, you can trade with anyone easily!
