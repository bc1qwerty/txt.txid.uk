## What did we learn?

Bitcoin is digital money created in 2009 by a person called Satoshi
Nakamoto. The coolest thing about it is that only 21 million Bitcoins
can ever exist! Just like gold, the amount is fixed so nobody can make
more. And if you have internet, you can send Bitcoin anywhere in the
world!
