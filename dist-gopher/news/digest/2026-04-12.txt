SpaceX Holds $603M Bitcoin as Corporate Treasury Adoption Accelerates
---------------------------------------------------------------------

SpaceX maintains a substantial Bitcoin position of 8,285 BTC ($603
million) despite the broader losses facing Elon Musk's xAI venture.
The holdings, secured through Coinbase Prime custody, demonstrate that
leading corporations continue treating Bitcoin as a legitimate
treasury asset in volatile market conditions.

Why it matters: Corporate adoption of Bitcoin as a store of value
validates the sound money thesis and signals institutional confidence
in Bitcoin's role as an alternative to fiat reserves amid economic
uncertainty.

Japan Reclassifies Bitcoin as Financial Instrument, Signaling
Regulatory Maturity
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Japan is moving to reclassify cryptocurrencies as financial products
under the Financial Instruments and Exchange Act, shifting Bitcoin
from a payment-based framework to one aligned with traditional
securities regulation. This change reflects growing acceptance of
Bitcoin's role as a financial asset across global markets.

Why it matters: National-level regulatory clarity for Bitcoin as a
financial instrument legitimizes it as sound money and removes
arbitrary barriers that previously confined it to alternative
channels.

→ Bitcoin Magazine
[https://bitcoinmagazine.com/news/japan-moves-to-classify-bitcoin-and-crypto]
