Trump Announces 30% Tariff on EU and Mexico
-------------------------------------------

President Trump announced a 30% tariff on goods from the European
Union and Mexico, effective August 1st. Both the EU and Mexico
expressed frustration, with the EU threatening countermeasures.

Why it matters: Trade wars historically drive capital toward
non-sovereign assets. Bitcoin's appeal as a neutral, borderless store
of value grows with every escalation.

Bitcoin ETF Inflows Hit $1.2B Weekly Record
-------------------------------------------

Spot Bitcoin ETFs recorded $1.2 billion in net inflows last week, the
highest since their January 2024 launch. BlackRock's IBIT led with
$780M.

Why it matters: Institutional adoption is no longer a narrative — it's
measurable capital flow. The ETF wrapper is pulling Bitcoin into
traditional portfolio allocation.

[https://www.bloomberg.com/]

Fed Signals Rate Cut Pause Amid Sticky Inflation
------------------------------------------------

Federal Reserve officials signaled a pause in rate cuts, citing
persistent core inflation at 3.2%. Markets now price only one cut for
the remainder of 2026.

Why it matters: Higher-for-longer rates squeeze leveraged positions
but strengthen Bitcoin's thesis as a long-term hedge against monetary
debasement.

→ Wall Street Journal [https://www.wsj.com/] · → Financial Times
[https://www.ft.com/]

El Salvador Launches Bitcoin Bond on Bitfinex Securities
--------------------------------------------------------

El Salvador officially listed its $1B "Volcano Bond" on Bitfinex
Securities, offering 6.5% annual yield backed by Bitcoin mining
revenue.

Why it matters: First sovereign Bitcoin-backed bond ever issued. If
successful, other emerging markets will follow — reshaping sovereign
debt markets.

→ Bitcoin Magazine [https://bitcoinmagazine.com/] · → The Block
[https://www.theblock.co/]

EU Begins MiCA Enforcement with Exchange Audits
-----------------------------------------------

The European Union kicked off MiCA enforcement, requiring all crypto
exchanges operating in the EU to complete compliance audits by Q3
2026.

Why it matters: Regulatory clarity is a double-edged sword —
compliance costs push out small players, but institutional confidence
grows with clear rules.
