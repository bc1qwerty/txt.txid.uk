Quantum Computing Threat Compresses Timeline for Bitcoin Protocol

----------------------------------------------------------------------

evolution than previously planned.

Why it matters: Bitcoin's security foundation depends on cryptographic

upgrades preserve Bitcoin's core function as immutable,
